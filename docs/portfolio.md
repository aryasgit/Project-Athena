# The Decision Intelligence Initiative

Athena is the third system in a three-part portfolio exploring one question from
three angles: **how are complex decisions made, executed, and optimised under
uncertainty?**

```
            ATHENA   ·  Enterprise Decision Intelligence   ·  business decisions
              ▲
            VEGA     ·  Quantitative Decision Intelligence ·  financial decisions
              ▲
            AGORA    ·  Electronic Market Infrastructure   ·  execution systems
```

- **Agora — Execution Intelligence.** How do markets execute decisions? The
  machinery of an exchange: orders, liquidity, price formation, market structure.
- **Vega — Quantitative Decision Intelligence.** How do we make better financial
  decisions? A research platform for evaluating strategies, risk, and edge.
- **Athena — Enterprise Decision Intelligence.** How should organisations decide
  what to do next? Enterprise data transformed into strategic recommendations.

The three are not separate projects; they are layers of the same problem —
turning large volumes of noisy data into reliable decisions.

## Resume framing

> Developed an enterprise decision-intelligence platform integrating a Postgres
> star-schema warehouse, a Python analytics and forecasting engine, and a
> Next.js executive dashboard to generate actionable, evidence-backed
> recommendations across HR, finance, and operational data.

## What Athena demonstrates

- **Data engineering** — modular ETL, star-schema modelling, bulk loading.
- **Analytics** — EDA, hypothesis testing, ranking, time-series forecasting.
- **Business intelligence** — KPI design, executive dashboards, data storytelling.
- **Consulting thinking** — every analysis resolves to a decision, with impact
  and a recommended action.
